# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a professional, respectful, and harassment-free environment
for contributors and reviewers.

## Expected behavior

- Keep discussion technical and respectful.
- Be explicit about uncertainty, risk, and tradeoffs.
- Protect private information, credentials, and security-sensitive details.
- Review AI-assisted output before publishing it.
- Correct mistakes transparently.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Knowingly submitting malicious, deceptive, plagiarized, or unsafe content.
- Public disclosure of exploitable security details before remediation.

## Reporting and enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to `OgeonX@gmail.com`. Maintainers may remove
content or restrict participation when necessary to protect the project and its
community spaces.
